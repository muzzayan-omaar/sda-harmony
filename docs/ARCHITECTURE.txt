MISSION
--------
Build the world's leading Seventh-day Adventist music streaming platform, starting in Uganda and expanding across Africa and eventually worldwide.

The platform connects:

• Listeners
• Choirs
• Solo Artists
• Music Ministries
• Conference Music Departments
• Union Music Departments
• Donors
• Platform Administrators

==========================================================
VERIFIED CHOIR SYSTEM
==========================================================

Every choir receives its own profile page.

Profile includes

• Choir Logo
• Banner
• Biography
• Members
• Albums
• Songs
• Followers
• Donation Button
• Analytics
• Social Media Links
• Contact Information

Verified Choir Badge

✓ Shows authenticity.

Users can

• Follow Choir
• Receive Notifications
• Donate
• Browse Albums
• Listen to Songs
• Share Choir Profile
